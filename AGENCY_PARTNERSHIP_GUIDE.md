# MBTQ.dev Agency Partnership Program

## Overview

MBTQ.dev is proud to partner with LGBTQIA and disability service agencies worldwide to provide comprehensive VR (Vocational Rehabilitation) and workforce development services. Our partnership program enables agencies to refer their clients to specialized services including business development, job training, assistive technology, and PinkSync ASL communication support.

## Mission Statement

We are committed to being the trusted partner for LGBTQ agencies across the globe, helping them fulfill their mission of supporting clients who are disabled, have specific accessibility needs, or require specialized workforce services.

## Partnership Benefits

### For Partner Agencies

- **Comprehensive Service Access**: Your clients gain access to the full suite of MBTQ.dev services
- **VR Business Development**: State-funded vocational rehabilitation business services
- **Workforce Training**: Job training and placement with inclusive employers
- **PinkSync ASL Support**: Professional ASL interpretation and communication services
- **Business Formation**: Entity formation and business registration assistance
- **Assistive Technology**: Technology assessment and accommodation services
- **Client Tracking Dashboard**: Monitor client progress and service delivery
- **Dedicated Support**: Assigned VR counselors for your clients
- **Global Network**: Connect with other partner agencies and share best practices

### For Clients

- **Inclusive Services**: LGBTQIA-friendly and disability-aware service delivery
- **Accessibility**: Full accessibility accommodations including ASL, assistive technology, and more
- **Comprehensive Support**: End-to-end support from idea to business launch or job placement
- **Professional Network**: Access to LGBTQIA and disability-friendly employers and mentors
- **Success Tracking**: Monitor your progress through personalized dashboards

## Eligibility

### Agency Requirements

Partner agencies should:
- Serve LGBTQIA, disabled, or workforce development populations
- Have active 501(c)(3) status or equivalent (for non-profit agencies)
- Maintain client data privacy and confidentiality standards
- Commit to referring qualified clients to MBTQ.dev services
- Provide feedback and outcome data to measure program effectiveness

### Client Eligibility

Clients referred by partner agencies may include:
- LGBTQIA individuals seeking employment or business opportunities
- Disabled individuals eligible for VR services
- Deaf or Hard of Hearing individuals requiring ASL support
- Individuals requiring specialized accommodations or support
- Job seekers from underserved communities
- Aspiring entrepreneurs from marginalized backgrounds

## How to Become a Partner

### Registration Process

1. **Submit Partnership Application**
   - Complete the online partnership form
   - Provide agency documentation (501(c)(3), etc.)
   - Describe your target population and services
   
2. **Partnership Review**
   - MBTQ.dev reviews application (typically 5-7 business days)
   - May request additional information or clarification
   
3. **Partnership Agreement**
   - Review and sign partnership agreement
   - Receive agency portal access credentials
   - Attend partnership orientation webinar
   
4. **Go Live**
   - Begin referring clients
   - Access agency dashboard and tools
   - Connect with MBTQ.dev support team

### Partnership Tiers

**Standard Partnership (Free)**
- Client referral portal access

- Quarterly reporting

**Premium Partnership**

**Enterprise Partnership**

## Referring Clients

### Referral Process

1. **Log into Agency Portal**
   - Access at [partnership.mbtq.dev](https://partnership.mbtq.dev)
   
2. **Create Client Referral**
   - Enter client information
   - Select services needed
   - Indicate special accommodations required
   - Provide referral context
   
3. **Client Intake**
   - Client receives welcome email and portal access
   - Completes intake forms and assessments
   - Assigned to appropriate VR counselor
   
4. **Service Delivery**
   - Services begin based on client needs
   - Progress tracked in real-time
   - Regular updates to referring agency
   
5. **Outcome Tracking**
   - Service completion documented
   - Outcomes and feedback collected
   - Success metrics shared with agency

### Required Information

For each client referral, please provide:
- Client name and contact information
- Preferred communication method
- Special needs/accommodations
- Services requested
- Reason for referral
- Any relevant background information
- Consent for information sharing
